Our Story

Founded in 1995, Sunset Hotel & Resort has been a beacon of luxury and hospitality for nearly three decades. What began as a small boutique hotel has blossomed into one of Miami's most prestigious destinations, known for its exceptional service and stunning oceanfront location.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a haven where guests could experience the perfect blend of comfort, elegance, and authentic hospitality. Over the years, we've hosted countless memorable moments, from romantic getaways to grand celebrations, each adding to our rich tapestry of stories.

Today, Sunset Hotel stands as a testament to timeless luxury, having undergone several thoughtful renovations while preserving its classic charm. Our commitment to excellence has earned us numerous accolades, including the prestigious "Best Luxury Hotel" award for five consecutive years.

As we look to the future, we remain dedicated to providing our guests with unforgettable experiences, maintaining the perfect balance between modern amenities and classic elegance that has become our signature.
